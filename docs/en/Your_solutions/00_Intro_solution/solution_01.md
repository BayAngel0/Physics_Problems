## Given

$$
\vec{a} = [2,1,-3], \quad \vec{b} = [4,-2,1]
$$

---

## a) Magnitude of each vector

$$
|\vec{a}|=\sqrt{2^2+1^2+(-3)^2}=\sqrt{4+1+9}=\sqrt{14}
$$

$$
|\vec{b}|=\sqrt{4^2+(-2)^2+1^2}=\sqrt{16+4+1}=\sqrt{21}
$$

---

## b) Dot Product

$$
\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b} = (2)(4) + (1)(-2) + (-3)(1)
$$

$$
= 8 - 2 - 3 = 3
$$

---

## c) Cross Product

Determinant form:

$$
\vec{a}\times\vec{b}
=
\begin{vmatrix}
i & j & k \\
2 & 1 & -3 \\
4 & -2 & 1
\end{vmatrix}
$$

Calculation:

$$
\vec{a}\times\vec{b}
$$

## i(1\cdot 1 - (-3)(-2))

j(2\cdot 1 - (-3)(4))
+
k(2\cdot (-2) - 1\cdot 4)
$$

$$
= i(1 - 6) - j(2 + 12) + k(-4 - 4)
$$

$$
= -5i - 14j - 8k
$$

Vector form:

$$
\vec{a}\times\vec{b} = [-5,-14,-8]
$$

---

## d) Angle Between the Vectors

Formula:

$$
\cos\theta=\frac{\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b}}{|\vec{a}||\vec{b}|}
$$

Substitute values:

$$
\cos\theta=\frac{3}{\sqrt{14}\sqrt{21}}=\frac{3}{\sqrt{294}}
$$

Angle:

$$
\theta=\cos^{-1}\left(\frac{3}{\sqrt{294}}\right)\approx 79.9^\circ
$$
