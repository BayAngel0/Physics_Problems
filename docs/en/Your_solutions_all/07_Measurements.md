## 1. Propagation of Error I

Radius:

$$
r = (6.20 \pm 0.05)\text{ cm}
$$

Volume of a sphere:

$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3
$$

Calculated volume:

$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi (6.20)^3 = 998.86\text{ cm}^3
$$

Uncertainty propagation:

$$
\frac{\Delta V}{V} = 3\frac{\Delta r}{r}
$$

$$
\frac{\Delta V}{V} = 3\left(\frac{0.05}{6.20}\right) = 0.02419
$$

Absolute uncertainty:

$$
\Delta V = 0.02419 \times 998.86 = 24.16
$$

Final result:

$$
V = (998.86 \pm 24.16)\text{ cm}^3
$$

## 2. Propagation of Error II

Measurements:

$$
L = (15.3 \pm 0.1)\text{ cm}
$$

$$
W = (8.4 \pm 0.1)\text{ cm}
$$

Area:

$$
A = L \times W
$$

$$
A = 15.3 \times 8.4 = 128.52\text{ cm}^2
$$

Uncertainty propagation:

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A} =
\frac{\Delta L}{L} + \frac{\Delta W}{W}
$$

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A} =
\frac{0.1}{15.3} + \frac{0.1}{8.4}
= 0.01844
$$

Absolute uncertainty:

$$
\Delta A = 128.52 \times 0.01844 = 2.37
$$

Final result:

$$
A = (128.52 \pm 2.37)\text{ cm}^2
$$

## 3. Propagation of Error III

Ohm’s law:

$$
R = \frac{V}{I}
$$

Measurements:

$$
V = (10.0 \pm 0.2)\text{ V}
$$

$$
I = (2.00 \pm 0.05)\text{ A}
$$

Resistance:

$$
R = \frac{10.0}{2.00} = 5.00\Omega
$$

Relative uncertainty:

$$
\frac{\Delta R}{R} =
\frac{\Delta V}{V} +
\frac{\Delta I}{I}
$$

$$
\frac{\Delta R}{R} =
\frac{0.2}{10.0} + \frac{0.05}{2.00}
= 0.045
$$

Absolute uncertainty:

$$
\Delta R = 5.00 \times 0.045 = 0.225
$$

Final result:

$$
R = (5.00 \pm 0.23)\Omega
$$

## 7. Standard Deviation

Scores:

$$
88,92,79,85,95,81,86,90,83,77,89
$$

Mean:

$$
\bar{x} = \frac{1}{11}\sum x_i
$$

$$
\bar{x} = 85
$$

Standard deviation:

$$
\sigma =
\sqrt{\frac{1}{N-1}\sum (x_i-\bar{x})^2}
$$

$$
\sigma \approx 5.58
$$

## 10. Light Speed Measurement

Microwave frequency:

$$
f = 2.45 \times 10^9 \text{ Hz}
$$

Measured distance between melted spots:

$$
d = 6.12\text{ cm}
$$

Wavelength:

$$
\lambda = 2d
$$

$$
\lambda = 0.1224\text{ m}
$$

Speed of light:

$$
c = f\lambda
$$

$$
c = (2.45 \times 10^9)(0.1224)
$$

$$
c = 2.99 \times 10^8\text{ m/s}
$$

Accepted value:

$$
3.00 \times 10^8\text{ m/s}
$$

Percentage error:

$$
\frac{|3.00 - 2.99|}{3.00} \times 100
$$

$$
= 0.33\%
$$
