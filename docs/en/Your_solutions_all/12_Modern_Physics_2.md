$$

$$

$$

$$

$$

$$
9 \text{ spatial states}

# 2. Energy Scaling

### a) Infinite Potential Well

Energy levels:

$$
E_n = \frac{n^2 h^2}{8mL^2}
$$

Thus:

$$
E_n \propto n^2
$$

---

### b) Hydrogen Atom

Energy levels:

$$
E_n = -\frac{13.6}{n^2} \text{ eV}
$$

Thus:

$$
E_n \propto \frac{1}{n^2}
$$

# 10. Wavefunction Probability

Wavefunction:

$$
\Psi(x) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{L}}\sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{L}\right)
$$

Probability density:

$$
P(x) = |\Psi(x)|^2
$$

### Region 1

$$
0 \le x \le L/4
$$

Probability:

$$
P \approx 0.09
$$

---

### Region 2

$$
L/4 \le x \le L/2
$$

Probability:

$$
P \approx 0.41
$$

Thus:

$$
L/4 \le x \le L/2
$$

is **more likely to contain the particle**.
